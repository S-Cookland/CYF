Come for a visit

Located on the East coast of North Yorkshire, it bears a 12th-century castle. You will find lots to do, from visiting local attractions to getting closer to nature at SEA LIFE and Peasholm Park. The open-air theatre has lots on offer from theatrical entertainment to live concerts. You can get by on the local Victorian train links, public transport, also there are lots of parking areas if you wish to drive around. The beaches are clean and family friendly with lots to do on the front and at the harbour, such as coastal trails, taking a Pirateship tour or a speedboat ride if you want to go faster!
